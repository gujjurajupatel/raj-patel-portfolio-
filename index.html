I'm Raj Vijaybhai Patel, a beginner film and TV actor from Nadiad, Gujarat. I'm at the start of my acting journey — learning on every set, every audition, and every scene I get to be part of.

Acting found me through curiosity and persistence rather than a head start. What I lack in screen credits, I bring in discipline, willingness to learn, and a hometown work ethic that doesn't quit.
